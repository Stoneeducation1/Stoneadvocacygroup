About Us

Born from frustration. Built for families.

We started Stone Advocacy Group because we know firsthand what it feels like to fight for a child in a system that wasn't designed to make it easy. We've sat in those meetings. We've read the federal law. We know what schools are required to do — and what they can't get away with.

Our advocates are trained in IDEA, Section 504, and early childhood special education law. We don't replace attorneys — we work alongside you to make sure your child's rights are protected at every step before and during the formal process.

We believe early intervention changes lives. The research is clear. The law is on your side. You just need someone who knows how to use it.
